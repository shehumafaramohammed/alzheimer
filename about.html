Biography

Shehu Mohammed is a PhD research scholar in Computer Applications at Lovely Professional University, India, under the sponsorship of the TETFund initiative. He hails from Talata Mafara in Zamfara State, Nigeria, and currently serves as a senior Lecturer and immediate past Head of the Department of Computer Science at Abdu Gusau Polytechnic, Talata Mafara. With over 15 years of professional experience spanning academia and the telecommunications industry, he has held multiple leadership positions, including Deputy Director of MIS and Chairman of the HODs Forum.

He holds a M.Sc. in Information Technology from the National Open University of Nigeria, a Postgraduate Diploma from Bayero University Kano, and a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Usmanu Danfodiyo University Sokoto.

Research Focus

Mr. Mohammed’s doctoral research focuses on the early detection of Alzheimer’s disease using multi-model machine learning techniques, leveraging multimodal data such as neuroimaging, clinical assessments, and behavioral biomarkers. His current work explores hybrid models involving Gradient Boosting Machines (GBM), Deep Neural Networks (DNNs), and Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) to enhance predictive performance and interpretability.
